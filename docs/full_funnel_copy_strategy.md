# Full Funnel Copy Strategy

## Narrative Spine

The site should read like a calm professional helping a person choose care, not like a curator explaining how the website is arranged.

The dominant pattern across the funnel is:

1. recognition
2. orientation
3. relevance
4. benefit
5. next step

## Tone Rules

Keep:

- calm
- mature
- respectful
- helpful
- human
- specific

Avoid:

- loud beauty-marketing clichés
- inflated promises
- medical claims
- internal UX language
- commentary about site architecture
- generic “brand presentation” phrasing

## Page Roles

### Homepage

- help a new visitor recognize their skin situation
- reduce confusion fast
- point either to `series.html` or directly to `catalog.html`
- support trust without turning into an editorial about the brand

### Series Page

- help a person identify the closest care direction
- speak from symptoms, states, and daily situations
- make category choice feel simpler and lower-risk

### Catalog Page

- serve people who already have a likely direction
- support comparison by format, price, and card detail
- keep a soft escape hatch back to selection by skin state

### Care Landing Pages

- support SEO entry from problem-led queries
- confirm that the visitor is in the right place
- show what to open first inside the direction

### Product Pages

- explain what the formula is for
- give concrete expectations without medical overclaiming
- help the person either add to cart or move within the same direction

### About Page

- justify trust
- connect the founder story to practical customer value
- move readers back into selection or catalog without pushiness

### Legal Pages

- reduce purchase friction
- clarify the order process calmly

## CTA Rules

Good CTA pattern:

- about the next useful action
- short
- concrete
- calm

Preferred CTA vocabulary:

- `Подобрать по коже`
- `Посмотреть направление`
- `Смотреть средства направления`
- `Открыть карточку`
- `Подробнее о средстве`
- `Уточнить подбор`

Avoid CTA vocabulary that sounds like system mechanics:

- `Перейти к серии`
- `Вернуться к подборке`
- `Карточка товара`
- `Назад к серии`

## Copy Rules For Customer States

Lead with conditions people can recognize:

- сухость и стянутость
- жирный блеск
- реакция после бритья
- нехватка мягкого водного этапа
- потребность в мягком очищении
- локальная сухость рук, губ, пяток

Do not over-explain classification.
Use state recognition to narrow choice naturally.

## Copy Rules For Trust

Brand trust should not sound like:

- “here is our philosophy”
- “here is our system”
- “here is how our architecture works”

Brand trust should sound like:

- here is why this person makes care carefully
- here is what that means for your skin and your choice

## Product-Layer Rules

Product text should answer:

- what is this for
- what kind of comfort or effect does it support
- how is it used
- what matters before ordering

Remove:

- source provenance notes
- internal references to Tilda or original source pages
- catalog-maintenance language

## Consistency Rules

Every meaningful page should make the next step obvious:

- homepage -> directions or catalog
- series -> care landing or example product
- care landing -> product page
- product page -> cart or related products in the same direction
- about -> directions or catalog

The site-wide voice should stay stable:

- first address the person
- then explain the direction
- only then explain supporting details
